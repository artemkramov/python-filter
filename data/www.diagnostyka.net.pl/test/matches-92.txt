The cross-correlation function has been applied to the study of the similarity of the two functions. One of these functions is known and represents a defect type HC (Head Checking) in the railway rail. The second function is unknown and based on cross-correlation function of both functions specifies the parameters of the similarities. These functions correspond to defects whose images have been determined using laser scatterometry method.
