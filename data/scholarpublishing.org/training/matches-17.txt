Nuclear Magnetic Resonance Spectroscopy Studies of Human Immunoglobulin ‘ G’ In Duchene Muscular Dystrophy
Sanjeev Kumar Mittal, Ms Sweety
<p>In the present paper, we have studied human Immunoglobulin G (IgG) in Duchene muscular dystrophy using nuclear magnetic resonance spectroscopy. A comparison with normal controls is also made. Some of the groups like phenylalanine ( -CH) , cysteine , serine&nbsp; are not found in DMD patients . These groups are found in healthy controls. A group Leuicine ( -CH) was appeared in cases only . NMR spectroscopy is a powerful tool to detect the chemical groups of amino acids in serum.&nbsp;</p>
