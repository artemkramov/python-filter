Neurological Disorders Summit (NDS-2015) concluded successfully on July 08, 2015. The three day summit was organized by United Scientific Group during July 06-08, 2015 at DoubleTree By Hilton, San Francisco, USA. The event was attended by speakers and delegates from more than 25 countries of the globe. The theme of NDS-2015 was Improvising Advanced Tools and Techniques for Better Therapeutics. The summit witnessed 8 keynote presentations, around 80 oral presentations and 30 poster presentations on various topics including Alzheimer’s disease, Parkinson’s disease, biomarkers, autism, stroke and other neurological disorders.</p><div><div
