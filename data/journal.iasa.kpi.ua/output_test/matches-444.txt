Classification of methods for risk measures VaR and CVaR calculation and estimation
Nataliia G. Zrazhevska, A. G. Zrazhevsky
A systematic classification of the existing approaches for popular risk measures VaR and CVaR calculating and estimating is fulfilled. A review of the most used methods is done. For convenience, the considered methods are reduced to common econometric designations and concepts, guidance on the use of the methods is proposed.
