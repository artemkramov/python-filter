Hierarchic planning with network representation of technological processes and limited resources as a Decision Making Problem
M. Z. Zgurovsky, A. A. Pavlov
The effective planning problem in systems with network representation of technological processes and limited resources is researched. The use of hierarchic planning models is grounded. It has been shown that even the effective problem solution by one scalar criterion is only the first stage of effective planning realization as a decision making problem with a poorly formalized global goal.
