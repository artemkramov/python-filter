Economic optimization problems in data security systems
E. G. Levchenko, R. B. Prus
The mathematical model and method of determination of the optimal distribution of the resources between the objects of data security are developed. A criterion function on the basis of which the illustrative calculations in a system of two information objects were done, is formulated. The directions of the proposed method development are outlined.
