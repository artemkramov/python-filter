Criteria and methods of fuzzy sets comparison
Yu. A. Zak
The criteria and methods are developed to determine the relative preference, to compare and rank the performance of a decision represented by fuzzy sets. The choice of a specific algorithm for comparing and ranking operations &nbsp;is determined by the core of the &nbsp;problem and the subjective preferences of ta decision maker. The approaches are developed on the basis of a comparison of a&nbsp;group of indicators that guarantee an obtaining the desired result, as well as multi-criteria models for solving the formulated problem. The developed models and methods can be widely used in the portfolio selection problems, &nbsp;in the prospective production planning, &nbsp;fuzzy regression analysis and mathematical programming &nbsp;problems with fuzzy data.
