Methodology of scenarios development for Ukraine using SWOT-analysis
M. Zgurovsky, K. Pereverza
The article describes the method of constructing scenarios for Ukraine using indices and indicators of sustainable development, SWOT-analysis, expert methods. Scenarios for short, medium and long term periods are presented.
