Modelling of the civilizations’ break lines in context of their fundamental cultural differences
М. Zgurovsky, А. Pasichny
Given article describes a development of a methodology of world geopolitics research with the use of contemporary geopolitical paradigms based on the work of expert groups in definition of the examined objects, development the set of evaluation criteria, and quantative estimation of the cultural differences of different objects. Current research was based on hypothesis of S. Hantington about the "clash of civilizations".
