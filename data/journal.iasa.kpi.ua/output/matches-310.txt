Modified method for analysis of hierarchies
M. Z. Zgurovsky, A. A. Pavlov, A. S. Shtankevich
An efficient methodology is proposed for searching for a winner among a great number of alternatives in the Analytic Hierarchy Process using optimization models of object priority searching according to empirical pairwise comparison matrixes. Two different models of empirical pairwise comparison matrixes are considered. A demonstrative example is given.
