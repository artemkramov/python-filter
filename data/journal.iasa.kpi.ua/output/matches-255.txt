Algebraic characteristic of the graph modifiers class
I. V. Redko, N. N. Snigur
Primitive program algebras of multiplace functions at a set of finite graphs were studied. Аn algebraic characteristic of the graph modifier class is given. The presented results are additional for the results previously obtained for vector, matrix, relational and table functions.
