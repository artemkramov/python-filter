Mathematic model for calculation of thermostressed state of shell constructs
E. V. Moskaleva
A mathematic model for calculation of thermostressed state of shell constructs is proposed as an improved theory of anisotropic shells which is built using the method of hypotheses. The thermostressed state of a shell construct is investigated taking into account the experimental data obtained.
