Fuzzy arithmetic in problems of interindustry balance
M. S. Siavavko, T. V. Pasichnyk, V. P. Tymkiv
To solve problems of interindustry balance, the use of fuzzy-step theory is proposed. Fuzzy logic methods are shown to enable quantitative interpretation of qualitative factors. To find a normal solution to incorrectly-assigned tasks or ill-conditioned systems, the use of the fractionally rational algorithm is offered. Examples of solutions to fuzzy problems are given.
