Principles for creation of automated system of expert tariff estimation for services on water delivery and drainage
P. I. Anpilogov, V. M. Mykhailenko
The efficient formation of tariff services on water delivery and drainage is considered. The creation and introduction of the automated tariff estimation for this problem solution are offered. Special technical means for control and measurement of basic technological parameters are required for the system to be realized. Definition of these parameters is a necessary condition for formation of the objective values of the tariff cost component.
