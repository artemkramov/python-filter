Formation of expert subgroups based on consensus and deriving generalized estimate of multiattribute objects
N. V. Krapuhina, S. V. Pronichkin, A. S. Rykov
New algorithms for handling expert estimates of multiattribute objects are considered and used for estimation of the competences of students estimated by several experts using many criteria. The application of the developed algorithms helps to make a more adequate decision to preferences of the PMD compared to the existing algorithms.
