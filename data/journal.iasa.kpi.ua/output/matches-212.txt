A projection-iteration algorithm of solution the ill-posed integral equations of Volter
L. L. Hart
The problem of applying the projection-iteration approach to the solution of ill-posed integral equations of Volter of the first kind is investigated. The comparative analysis of the suggested computational schemes, which use the various ways of a regularization parameter choice is carried out, the practical convergence of these schemes for solving concrete problems is demonstrated.
