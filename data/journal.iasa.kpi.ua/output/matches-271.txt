Scenario method for analysis of complex social systems
K. Pereverza
The article describes the possibilities and the ways of application of scenario method for analysis of complex social systems (such as countries, cities, companies, organizations). The short history of scenario method is given. Definitions of scenario, typology and research of scenario, recommendations concerning scenario organization and implementation considered.
