Environment sustainability assessment at sub-national level in Ukraine
M. Z. Zgurovsky, G. O. Statyukha, I. M. Dzhygyrey
The existing methods for assessment of the environment sustainable development (independent indicators, their systems and indices) are considered. The environment sustainability index for assessment of relations with the environment at a regional level for Ukraine is proposed, which takes into account the national priorities in ecological policy. Ecological profiles and rating of the Ukrainian regions are obtained according to the proposed regional index.
