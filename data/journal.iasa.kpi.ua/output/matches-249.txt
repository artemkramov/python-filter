Method of quantative answers assessment in the knowledge testing system of open type
I. V. Zamarueva, L. M. Badorina
Method of students knowledge assessment is considered with the help of comparative analysis of textual answer with a given standard text and determination of their relevance. There are given practical settlements. Particular attention is given to the research of the linguistic model of knowledge representation on the basis of term synonymy with subject area.
