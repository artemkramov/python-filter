Single retrial lossless queueing system with Erlang distribution of orbit cycle time
S. V. Pustova
A single retrial lossless queueing system with Erlang distribution of the call time on the orbit cycle is considered. An analytical model for the system of the M / M / 1 / 0 /// E<sub>2</sub>type is developed, and an algorithm for calculation of the system performance characteristics is constructed. The results obtained are analyzed.
