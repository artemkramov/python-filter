Open-close principles of the integration environments
I. V. Redko, O. O. Tatarikov, M. P. Semenchenko
Within the concept of OC-System the concepts of the integration environment and integration system are considered. The solutions of representative examples of problems in macro- and microintegration environments are considered. The application of the method of the evolution enrichment to essences of business process type are described.
