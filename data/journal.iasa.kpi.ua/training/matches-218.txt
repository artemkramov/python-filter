Computer-based modeling of Ukrainian sigh language
Iu. G. Kryvonos, Iu. V. Krak, O. V. Barmak
A mathematical model and its software implementation for sign language speech obtaining and reproduction using 3d human model has been suggested. The technology of enough realistically reproduction of 3d model of the traffic received from the motion picture of the particular person — the bearer of the sigh language, — is proposed.
