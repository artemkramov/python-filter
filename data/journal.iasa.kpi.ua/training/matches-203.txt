Evaluation of sensitivity of the DS/AHP method to the applying in the most of the alternatives
N. I. Nedashkovskaya
The formalization of the integrated decision support method DS/AHP by many criteria with incomplete expert estimates, which combines the method of hierarchies’ analysis and the theory of confidence of Dempster-Shafer, is done. Evaluation of the sensitivity of the rankings, obtained by the DS/MAI method to changes in the set of solutions alternatives, is carried out. Changes of rankings are illustrated with a few examples.
