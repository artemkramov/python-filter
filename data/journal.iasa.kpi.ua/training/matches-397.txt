Dynamic model of intersector balance taking into account control of contamination without lags
М. V. Boichuk, N. M. Shmurygina
A model of dynamic intersector balance taking into account control of contamination is considered. The investigation of it has resulted in offering algorithms of calculation of the main values of capital, investments, non productive consumption, labour resources, gross output of basic products and products of auxiliary production, eventual products, regional managements — values of investments, as well as points of switching of investment managements.
