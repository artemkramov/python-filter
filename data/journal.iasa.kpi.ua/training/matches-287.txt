Optimization of telecommunication network fragment
K. S. Sunduchkov
A method for analysis and determination of telecommunication network parameters is developed, and their mathematic models are constructed along with determination of the parameter domains and ranges of independent variables. A method for optimization and criterion of optimization are presented. An example of searching for optimal parameters of telecommunication network is provided.
