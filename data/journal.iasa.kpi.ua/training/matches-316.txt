To modeling of stochastic situations in decision making
V. І. Ivanenko, V. M. Mikhalevich
The definitions of lottery and matrix models of the decision making situation with a stochastic cause-effect mechanism are given. It is shown that these models are equivalent for the simulation of broad class of such situations.
