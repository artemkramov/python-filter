Fractal "profiles" of schemes of collective remaining knowledge
V. V. Yasinsky
Collective knowledge is shown to be transformed with time into a stable information scheme of fractal configuration which can be interpreted as a phase "profile" of the scheme of collective remaining knowledge. The research is based on the systemic generalization of the results of long-term comprehensive monitoring of the teaching process quality in the NTUU "KPI".
