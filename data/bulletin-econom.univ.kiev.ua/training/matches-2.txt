The article highlights the historic features and modern trends of European orientation of Ukraine’s economy. The analyzed dynamics of export / import of Ukrainian goods and services to / from the eurozone countries and means of
stimulating export activities, in particular through the creation and implementation of innovative products. Was investigated the problems arising from the implementation of the strategic European principles in Ukraine.
