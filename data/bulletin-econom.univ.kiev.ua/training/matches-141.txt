Іn the article the theoretical fundamentals of the prudential banking supervision effectiveness and substantiation of approaches to calculation of the integral indicator of supervisory system compliance with the Basel Committee Core Principles were investigated.
The “functional effectiveness” and “institutional effectiveness” concepts of supervisory activity were suggested.
