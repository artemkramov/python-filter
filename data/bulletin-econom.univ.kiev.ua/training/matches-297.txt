This article presents a review of capital structure studies paradigm, founded by the Miller- Modigliani theorem. The research includes a brief view on origins of capital structure issue, importance of the Miller- Modigliani theorem for
modern research, and current agenda of modern studies. The article also suggests post-soviet scholars to adopt and use modern theories and methodology.</p><p>Keywords: capital structure; firm capital; financing sources; modern finance theory; corporate finance studies</p><p>Date of submission 26.07.16</p><p>DOI:&nbsp;<a href="https://doi.org/10.17721/1728-2667.2016/185-8/6">https://doi.org/10.17721/1728-2667.2016/185-8/6</a></p><p
