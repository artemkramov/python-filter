maxim" title="View all posts by Maxim">Maxim
The paper studies the impact of state aid instruments on the development of agriculture in Ukraine. The research identifies the main trends of the Ukrainian agricultural sector and presents econometric
models of the state aid impact on the development of agriculture in Ukraine. The author makes recommendations for improvement of budget resources spending on agricultural development in Ukraine.</p><p>Key words: agriculture; state aid; modeling; state aid instruments.</p><p>Date of submission 09.12.2015</p><p>DOI:&nbsp;<a href="http://dx.doi.org/10.17721/1728-2667.2016/178-1/9">http://dx.doi.org/10.17721/1728-2667.2016/178-1/9</a></p><p
