1540" title="10:28 PM">Sunday November 1st, 2015
maxim" title="View all posts by Maxim">Maxim
The necessity of business activity insurance in the agricultural sector is grounded in this article. Different types of agricultural risks are described. The principles,
on which the efficiency of agricultural insurance is based, are determined. Such as: voluntary; the farmers interest; risk management; multivariate product line; promote farmers efficient; equal access to subsidies from farmers; balance; transparency and coordination of the subsidies transfer; creating a reliable and multi-level system of insurance coverage; binding institute of independent examination. The different ways to determine the effectiveness of insurance are considered.</p><p>Keywords: agricultural insurance; insurance efficiency; estimation of the insurance efficiency.</p><p>DOI:&nbsp;<a href="http://dx.doi.org/10.17721/1728-2667.2014/156-3/14">http://dx.doi.org/10.17721/1728-2667.2014/156-3/14</a></p><h5
