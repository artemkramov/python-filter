The article analyzes the structure of the potential of innovative development of the of ukrainian agroindustrial sector. There was studied scien­tific potential and agribusiness production capacity in
order to identify significant disparities hamper scientific and industrial cooperation.</p><p>The author considers the market and the state priorities of innovation and constructed cross-section. The recommendations for areas of improvement of the innovative development organizational-economic mechanism of the sector, which will enhance the competitiveness of agribusiness sector, providing a high level of scientific and technological development of the agricultural sector as one of the priorities in Ukraine.</p><p>Keywords: agro-industrial sector, the mechanism of innovation, government regulation, scientific and industrial cooperation, innovation potential.</p><p>Date of submission 11.02.2016</p><p>DOI:&nbsp;<a href="http://dx.doi.org/10.17721/1728-2667.2016/180-3/8">http://dx.doi.org/10.17721/1728-2667.2016/180-3/8</a></p><p
