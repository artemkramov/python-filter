PROSPECTS FOR THE CYCLING DEVELOPMENT IN IVANO-FRANKIVSK
Levytska О.</p><p>Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv, Kyiv, Ukraine
/strong></p><p>Analysis of the status and prospects of development of cycling on an example of Ivano-Frankivsk, determining the place of cycling in the city transport concept and its role in urban planning as a whole. Done mobile SWOT-analysis of the situation in Ivano-Frankivsk. Presented and analyzed the planned location of bicycle paths in Ivano-Frankivsk to the implementation of program activities Disclosed long term development of cycling, and form the basis for the Master Plan Cycling.</p><p><strong>Keywords:</strong>
cycling, transport communications, urban planning
