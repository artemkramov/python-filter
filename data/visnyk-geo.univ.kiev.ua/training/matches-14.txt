REGIONAL TOURISM POLICY REPUBLIC OF POLAND
Oliynyk Ya, Obodovska V.</p><p>Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv, Kyiv, Ukraine
/strong></p><p>The paper analyzed the newest tourist aspects of regional policy of the Republic of Poland. Investigated the basic normatively legal documents in the field of regional policy. Determined the main goals and objectives “Tourism Development Program until 2020”. Highlights the regional differences for the last eight years in the accommodation of foreign tourists in Poland.</p><p><strong>Keywords:</strong>
regional policy, tourism policy, tourism, socio-economic development, Tourism Development Program until 2020, Poland.
