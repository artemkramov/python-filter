CHARACTERISTICS OF THE TEMPERATURE REGIME OF THE KANEV IN THE CONTEMPORARY CLIMATE CONDITIONS
Scherban I.</p><p>Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv, Kyiv, Ukraine
/strong></p><p>In this work the climatic features of the mean monthly air temperature at meteorological stations Kaniv are considered. The air temperature changes over the past 20 years with climatological standard norm (period 1960-1991 years) are compared. The graphs of perennial changes in air temperature are built. The years with abnormal temperatures are defined.</p><p><strong>Keywords:</strong>
air temperature, perennial change of air temperature
