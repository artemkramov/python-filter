STATE AND MAIN TRENDS OF RESOURCE USE BY ECONOMY IN REGIONAL DIMENSION
Cherkaska O.</p><p>Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv, Kyiv, Ukraine
/strong></p><p>The level of natural resources consumption and their ratio with GDP levels are analyzed in regional espect. Regional differences and trends in cetain types of resources consumption are revealed in accordance with reasons determining them. The grouping of regions according to the ratio between GDP and resources consumtion is made. There is revealed the groupe of regions with high and very high levels of dependency on resources exploitation.</p><p><strong>Keywords:</strong>
natural resources, consumption of resources, regions, economy
