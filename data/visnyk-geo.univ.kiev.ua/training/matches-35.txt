METHODICAL FEATURES OF DATA INTERPRETATION OF REMOTE SENSING FOR GEOINFORMATION MAPPING OF CHERNIVTSI REGION LAND RESOURCES
Bondarenko E., Smirnov Ya.</p><p>Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv, Kyiv, Ukraine
/strong></p><p>Formulated automated and manual techniques of Landsat data interpretation on the basis of CORINE land resources classification system. Defines the main spectral characteristics of the land cover categories in the Chernivtsi region. Constructed vector polygonal layers with boundaries of land cover categories in region for the years 1989 and 2010.</p><p><strong>Keywords:</strong>
remote sensing, image classification, GIS, land resources, land cover, land use, CORINE
