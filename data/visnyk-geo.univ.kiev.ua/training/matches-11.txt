GEOINFORMATION MAPPING ALGORITHM OF CHERNIVTSI REGION LAND RESOURCES ON THE BASIS OF SPATIAL DATA INFRASTRUCTURES
Bondarenko E., Smirnov Yа.</p><p>Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv, Kyiv, Ukraine
/strong></p><p>In the article is formulated geoinformation mapping algorithm of land resources mapping on the basis of spatial data infrastructures (SDI). Defined major and minor elements of SDI needed for geoinformation mapping of land resources and the basic requirements to the sources and forms of their presentation. As a result of analysis of the SDI components, created model of their interaction and visualization provided in the forms of series of static and interactive maps.</p><p><strong>Keywords:</strong>
