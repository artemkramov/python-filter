ANALYSIS OF CONSUMER PREFERENCES OF HOLIDAYMAKERS ON THEIR SHORT SUMMER HOLIDAYS
Anisimov S.</p><p>Research establishment “Ukrainian Scientific Research Institute of Ecological Problems “, Kharkiv, Ukraine
/strong></p><p>Analysis of demand for recreational services carried out on the basis of the literature data and the results of a questionnaire survey. Motivations, expectations and interests of unorganized tourists on the coast of the S. Donets River in the summer of 2013-2014 was studied. Territorial preferences, the structure and the length of recreational activities have been identified. The characteristics of desirable indicators of natural-resource potential of the territory and economic infrastructure has been received.</p><p><strong>Keywords:</strong>
recreational services, questionnaire survey, territorial preferences of holidaymakers, structure and duration of the recreational activities
