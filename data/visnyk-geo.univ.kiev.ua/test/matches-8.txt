MODERN TRENDS IN MANAGEMENT OF ECONOMIC SECURITY COMPANY TRAVEL
Zarya І.</p><p>National aviation university, Kiev, Ukraine
/strong></p><p>Substantiates the main directions of the study of economic security of the tourist enterprise. The basic approach to the interpretation of the definition of “economic security”. The factors that should determine the vector of the basic provisions of the economic security of the tourist business. A number of effective measures to counter the negative impacts of socio-economic processes that affect the internal and external activities of the tourism enterprises and require constant monitoring and improvement.</p><p><strong>Keywords:</strong>
travel business, economic security, social and economic processes, management of economic security, the security state of enterprise
