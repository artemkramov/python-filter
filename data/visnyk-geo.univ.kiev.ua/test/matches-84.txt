ADJUSTING OF RECREATION STREAMS WITHIN THE LIMITS OF DESIGNED OF ECOPATH “ON THE WAVES OF TIRAS”
Babyuk L.
/strong>
Problems acaused increasing development of uncontrolled recreation activity within the limits of the protected landscapes of the Dnestr canyon are considered. Necessity of creation of ecopath as an effective instrument of optimization of the recreation use of territory are grounded. Recommendations from adjusting of tourist streams on ecopath are given.
