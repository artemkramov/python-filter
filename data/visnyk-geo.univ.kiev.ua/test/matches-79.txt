COMPETITIVE PRINCIPLES OF THE UKRAINIAN REGIONS EXPORT POTENTIAL FORMING
Oliynyk Ya. Zapototskyy S.
/strong>
The article characterizes theoretical and methodological principles of the forming of the competitive export capacity at the regional range. The estimation of foreign economic activity of ukrainian regions is made and a system of special insrtuments for the competitive export potential regions improving is being proposed.
