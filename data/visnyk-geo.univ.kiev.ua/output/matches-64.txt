PROCESSES OF TRANSPORT AND LOGISTICS CLUSTERIZATION IN EUROPEAN UNION AND UKRAINE: REGIONAL DIMENSION
Smyrnov I., Shmatok O.
/strong></p><p>Shown main features and characteristic touches of formation processes of transport and logistic clusters (TLC) in EU, in particular, their dividing into port, boundary, regional kinds; their co-operation which results in the emergence of new categories of TLC, e.c. multiports – gateways and butt ports; finally, as end-point, is creation of the European network of TLC with the European Core Region as its basis.</p><p><strong>Keywords:</strong>
transportation and logistics cluster Eurologistics, multiport – gateways, transshipment / interlining ports, the European network of TLC
