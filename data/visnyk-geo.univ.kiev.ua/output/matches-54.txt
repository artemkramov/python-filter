THE DEVELOPMENT OF TOURISM INDUSTRY IN THE BORDER TOWNS ON THE EXAMPLE OF STRASBOURG
Glybovets V., Saharchuk N.</p><p>Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv, Kyiv, Ukraine
/strong></p><p>Learned the main features of the development of tourist industry in Strasbourg. Identify the most important factors that coused the fast, versatile and efficient development of tourism in the capital of Alsace. Particular emphasis is placed on the border situation Strasbourg, from what can be a start in the development of guidelines for the development of tourism in the border and inland cities of Ukraine. The main priorities in the development of tourism Ukrainian towns and villages, such as investing in the reconstruction of historical and cultural dissemination of advertising on the Internet, creating favorable conditions of infrastructure, service were investigated. It surveyed 500 people for tourism development in Strasbourg, the results of which are described in the article.</p><p><strong>Keywords:</strong>
tourism economic development, tourist facilities, tourist region
