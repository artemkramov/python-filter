MERCURY IN KIEV REGION SOILS AND PHYTOCENOSISES,&nbsp;THE MECHANISM OF ITS ACCUMULATION AND DISTRIBUTION
Kovalchuk V.
/strong>
A general mechanism of distribution of mercury in soils is being shown. This article characterizes the content and distribution of the mercury in Kiev region soils. The effect of different doses of mercury on the plant growth is being studied based on the example of the barley growth.
