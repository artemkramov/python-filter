ELECTROMECHANICALLY TUNABLE DIELECTRIC MICROWAVE DEVICES
Yuriy Prokopenko, Yuriy Poplavko, Vitaliy Molchanov
<p>High-tunable low-loss microwave dielectric devices are designed by electromechanical alteration in the configuration of device&nbsp;components. Specifically, this device consists of dielectric and metallic parts with an air gap between them. The gap location&nbsp;provides the largest perturbation in the electromagnetic field and a mini-actuator controls the device. Through experiments&nbsp;conducted, we test and apply the high-tunable dielectric resonators and filters as well as wide-band low loss phase shifters.</p>
