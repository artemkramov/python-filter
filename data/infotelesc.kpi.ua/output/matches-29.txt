TWENTY INTERNATIONAL CRIMEAN CONFERENCES “MICROWAVE &amp; TELECOMMUNICATION TECHNOLOGY” (CriMiCo)
Mykhaylo Ilchenko, Pavlo Yermolov
<p>The International Crimean Microwave Conference (CriMiCo) is convened in Sevastopol since 1991. 20 years saw that this&nbsp;conference became the flagship venue for far reaching research into theoretical, experimental, manufacturing, applied and historical&nbsp;aspects of microwave engineering and telecommunication technology. Solely in 2010 over 500 reports were delivered&nbsp;by scientists and experts from 208 universities and 17 countries: Belarus, Belgium, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Germany, Israel,&nbsp;Iran, Kazakhstan, Canada, China, Korea, Lithuania, Moldova, Poland, Russia, USA, Ukraine and the Republic of South Africa.</p>
