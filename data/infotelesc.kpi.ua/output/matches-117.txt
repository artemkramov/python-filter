CAPACITY INCREASING OF SENSOR TELECOMMUNICATION NETWORKS
Oleksandr Lysenko, Stanislav Valuiskyi
<p>A method of capacity increasing of sensor telecommunication networks has been proposed and investigated. The method proposed&nbsp;is based on a placement control of unmanned aerial vehicles in view of the rapid and unpredictable movement of mobile&nbsp;subscribers. A distinctive feature of developed method is combining mathematical models of connectivity estimation taking&nbsp;into account a quality of service of mobile subscribers and an advanced search algorithm of quasi-optimal placement of unmanned&nbsp;aerial vehicles in a single computational procedure. An application of the method proposed allows increasing the&nbsp;throughput of telecommunication networks to 15─20 percent in comparison with existing methods. Deviations of obtained&nbsp;sub-optimal solutions from optimal ones received by exhaustive method do not exceed 5─7 percent.</p>
