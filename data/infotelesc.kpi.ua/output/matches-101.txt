ANALYSIS OF CORRECTIVE PROPERTIES OF ULTRA-LONG LDPC CODES
Leonid Uryvskyi, Serhii Osypchuk
<p>The ultra-long LPDC codes are examined. The errors correction by ultra-long LDPC codes is analyzed. The error-correcting&nbsp;capability research method for ultra-long LDPC codes is described in details. The main results in table and graph representations&nbsp;are shown. The comparison of ultra-long LDPC codes and BCH codes is performed by using Plotkin and Varshamov-Gilbert limits approximation criterion.</p>
