OPTIMAL CONTROL OF TELECOMMUNICATION AEROPLATFORM IN THE AREA OF EMERGENCY
Oleksandr Lysenko, Stanislav Valuiskyi, Pavel Kirchu, Anton Romaniuk
<p>This paper addresses to a method for increasing of mobile ad-hoc networks throughput based on the placement control of unmanned&nbsp;aerial vehicles in the area of emergency situation. A further development of this method, namely the improvement of&nbsp;UAV flight control subsystem, that will allow operative implementation of obtained in the previous stage location coordinates&nbsp;while minimizing energy consumption for control, is proposed. The proposed approach will improve network throughput by&nbsp;15–20% while reducing fuel costs by an average of 13–15%.</p>
