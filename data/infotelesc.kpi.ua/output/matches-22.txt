FAIRNESS FOR OPTIMIZATION IN COOPERATIVE WIRELESS CELLULAR NETWORKS
Maxime Guirnyk
<p>Cooperative communication can improve the performance of cellular mobile networks. The optimization techniques based on&nbsp;duality theory, decomposition and subgradient method were applied for such wireless cellular cooperative systems. It was&nbsp;shown that the joint optimization and resource allocation problem can be solved efficiently within a network utility maximization&nbsp;framework. A concept of proportional fairness was used to achieve fair distribution of quality of service among users. Simulation&nbsp;results confirm the validity of the theoretical work.</p>
