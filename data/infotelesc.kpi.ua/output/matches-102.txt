MULTILEVEL INTELLECTUAL APPROACH TO HTTP-REQUESTS LEGITIMACY VALIDATION
Volodymyr Kononenko, Serhii Kravchuk
<p>In the paper a multilevel intellectual approach to HTTP-requests legitimacy validation is proposed. The approach is devised for&nbsp;HTTP-flood DDoS-attacks detection and prevention in telecommunication networks with a web-server as the target attack object.&nbsp;The analysis of HTTP-requests attributes and their signatures is provided. On the basis of the analysis the attributes are&nbsp;separated into several levels that allow us to design a flow analyzer in a form of the multilevel block. Due to a multilevel structure&nbsp;of the flow analyzer a minimization of resources, spent for a request handling, is achieved.</p>
