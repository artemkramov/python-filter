MITRIS SYSTEM WITH COMBINED QUADRATURE-AMPLITUDE AND FREQUENCY MODULATION
Teodor Narytnyk
<p>The telecast and Internet distribution system, which use combined two-level modulation and intended for digital information&nbsp;transfer in frequency band 11,7─12,5 GHz was created on a base of the existing microwave integrated telecommunication system&nbsp;MІTRІS. In comparison with telecommunication system using 64-QAM modulation with one carrier, new system has significantly&nbsp;better power engineering, less hard requirements to the frequency stability, to the phase noise level and to the linearity&nbsp;of the amplitude-frequency and phase-frequency characteristics of communication channel.</p>
