ANALYSIS OF SIGNALS OF STABILIZED AUTODYNES
Vladislav Noskov, Kirill Ignatkov, Sergey Smolskiy
<p>Results of the autodyne signal analysis of the self-oscillating systems stabilized in frequency by the external high-quality cavity&nbsp;are given. The coupling between the main and stabilizing cavities is realized on the basis of a pass-reflective filter with a resistive&nbsp;link. Mathematical equations are obtained describing an autodyne response onto impact of the own radiated signal reflected&nbsp;from a target. The analysis of phase, amplitude, frequency and amplitude-frequency characteristics of the autodyne system&nbsp;is fulfilled. The calculation of an autodyne signal spectrum is discussed. A new type of nonlinear distortions of an autodyne&nbsp;signal is examined, which is caused by a frequency dispersion of an oscillating system of the stabilized autodyne. Advantages&nbsp;of the stabilized autodyne compared to the usual single-tuned autodyne oscillator are shown.</p>
