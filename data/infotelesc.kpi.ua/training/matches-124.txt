ANTENNA ON MICROSTRIP LINE WITH ORTHOGONALLY PLACED DIELECTRIC RESONATOR
Igor Trubarov
<p>Single-element antenna that uses microstrip line as a feeder and cylindrical dielectric resonator orthogonally oriented relative&nbsp;to the line as a radiating element has been investigated. The complete mathematical model of proposed design consisting of a&nbsp;number of analytical expressions for main antenna characteristics is obtained and verified. The analytical relation for antenna<br>return loss versus stub length and coupling coefficients of dielectric resonator with the feeding line and open space is derived.&nbsp;The assessment of the potential possibility and conditions of perfect antenna matching with feeding line is carried out. The&nbsp;influence of main parameters of a dielectric resonator antenna on its characteristics is examined. The numerical analysis of&nbsp;different antenna parameters proving the obtained analytical expressions is performed. The results of theoretical analysis are in&nbsp;good agreement with experimental data.</p>
