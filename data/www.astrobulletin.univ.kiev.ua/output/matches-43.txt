U-values of stars and galaxies from digitized astronomic negatives obtained by Baldone Schmidt telescope
I. Eglitis</strong>, Dr. phys.,<br><strong>M. Eglite</strong>, MSc.,<br>Institute of Astronomy, University of Latvia, Riga<br><strong>V. Andruk</strong>,Msc.,<strong><br>L. Pakuliak</strong>, Ph. D.<br>Astronomical Observatory NASU, Ukraine, Kyiv<br><span></span><br><strong>Abstract<br>
Digitizing and processing of several dozen Johnson U plates from the archive of 1.2 m Baldone Schmidt telescope were made. Scans were obtained using Epson Expression scanners. Image processing was made in the LINUX/MIDAS/ROMAFOT package. Obtained positional accuracy of Tycho2 stars is 0.1 ”. The precision of the characteristic curves restoration is 0.1–0.2&nbsp; m.
Keywords<br>
