An impact of nearby attractors on the collective peculiar motion of galaxies
S. Parnovsky</strong>, Dr. Sci., Prof.<br>Astronomical Observatory of Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv<br><span></span><br><strong>Abstract
<br>We study the impact of individual attractors on the multipole model of the collective motion of galaxies. Equations obtained provide a theoretical possibility to estimate masses and locations of attractors together with the parameters of the multipole model. Unfortunately, this is not possible on the state-of-the-art level of estimation of galaxy’ peculiar velocities.
Keywords
