<br></span>The present state of organizing, electronic cataloguing, digitizing and re-processing of the AO KNU collection of astronomical photographs were considered in the paper. The consideration was carried out in the framework of the national and the international project of the Virtual Observatory. It was discussed the position and the photometric accuracy of certain photographic plates, the search results for the optimal methods and the treatment approaches.
