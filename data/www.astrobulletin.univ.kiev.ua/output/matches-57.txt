How to find an initial luminosity functions of starburst galaxies
Parnovsky S.,</strong>Dr. Sci.,<br>Astronomical observatory of the Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv, Kyiv<br><span></span><br><strong>Abstract
<br>We show how to find the initial luminosity function (LF) which appear the distributions of galaxy luminosities at zero starburst age<em>L<sub>0</sub></em>from the current LF of the sample of the starburst galaxies. We solve the corresponding Integral equation and get the formula for initial LF<em>n<sub>1</sub>(L<sub>0</sub>)</em>obtained from the LF<em>n(L )</em>of the sample. In particular we consider the case of<em>n(L)</em>being the log-normal function
