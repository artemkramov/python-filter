Estimation of the combination of some cosmological parameters from large-scale motion of RFGC-galaxies
S. Parnovsky,</strong>Dr. Phys. and Math. Sci., Prof.<br>Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv, Kyiv<span></span></p><p><strong>Abstract
<br>A constrain on the combination of cosmological parameters Ωm and σ8 is obtained from the peculiar motion of RFGC galaxies. We find the estimation (Ωm/ 0.3)0.35σ8=0.87±0.05.
Keywords
<br>Large-scale motion of galaxies, cosmological parameters.
