Metric of the homogeneous anisotropic model of Bianchi type I with an arbitrary equation of state
S. L. Parnovsky</strong>, Dr. Sci,<br>Astronomical observatory of Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv<span></span></p><p><strong>Abstract
<br>We find the metric of the homogeneous anisotropic model of Bianchi type II with an arbitrary equation of state. Its particular cases are the Kasner metric, the Heckmann-Schücking one and the metric with the cosmological constant.
