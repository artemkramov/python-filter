Malacofauna of Tekkali Creek, Srikakulam District, Andhra Pradesh, India: Check list
The molluscan fauna of Tekkali Creek of Srikakulam district, Andhra Pradesh, India was recorded. About 22 species of molluscs (16 gastropods and 6 bivalves) were recorded. Among them two species were marine, eighteen brackish water and two fresh water
