Some Fixed Point Theorems in 2-Metric Spaces
In the present paper we prove two common fixed theorems for four mappings in complete 2-metric spaces. This theorem is a version of many fixed point theorems in complete metric spaces, given by many authors announced in the literature.
Vishal Gupta, S. B. Singh and Ravinder Kumar
