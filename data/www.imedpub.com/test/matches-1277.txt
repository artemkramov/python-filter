Fatty acid composition of Sapindus mukorossi seed oil
Fatty acid methyl ester (FAME) composition of Sapindus mukorossi seed oil was determined by IR, NMR and GCMS analysis. The FAME of the oil of Sapindus mukorossi consists of 4.78 wt.% of methyl palmitate (C16:0), 58.89 wt.% of methyl oleate (C18:1), 1.78 wt.% of methyl stearate (C18:0), 25.93 wt.% of methyl 11-trans eicosenoate (C 18:2), and 8.61 wt.% of methyl arachidate (C20:0).
Md. Abdul Halim Shah, Kajal Dutta and Dibakar Chandra Deka
