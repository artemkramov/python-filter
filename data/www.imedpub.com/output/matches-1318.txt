Fatty acid composition of Paederia foetida seed oil
Fatty acid methyl ester (FAME) composition of Paederia foetida seed oil was determined by IR, NMR and GC-MS analysis. FAME from Paederia foetida consists of 15.35 wt.% of methyl palmitate (C16:0), 47.70 wt.% of methyl linoleate (C18:2), 30.54 wt.% of methyl oleate (18:1) and 6.40 wt.% of methyl stearate (C18:0).
Md. Abdul Halim Shah, Kajal Dutta and Dibakar Chandra Deka
