Experimental and quantum chemical calculations of tris(morpholinyldithiocarbamato)antimony (III)
In this paper, vibrational analysis of tris(morpholinyldithiocarbamato)antimony(III) were presented using experimental techniques and quantum chemical calculations. HOMO-LUMO properties and First hyperpolarizability also discussed
