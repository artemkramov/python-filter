Problems of sense and sense-creating in the context of sociological theories by Niklas Luhman and Anthony Giddens
A. Dejneka
<p>Article describes sociological experience in studying of problematic of sense and sense-creating in view of Niklas Luhman's and Anthony&nbsp;Giddens' theories. Author problematizes varieties of conceptualization of this problem in classical sociology, formulates fundamentals of both&nbsp;viewed approaches to sense-creating problematic and delineates basic differences and parallels between them.</p></div><br></div><div><h4>Keywords</h4><br><div>sense; sense-creating; social systems theory.
