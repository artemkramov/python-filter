Ethnicity: Plurality of Interpretations
N. Butkovska
<p>The article singles out the factors that influenced the conceptual exploration of ethnic issues in the 20<sup>th</sup>century. One the main factors is new ethnicity manifestations resulting from the majority of modern societies transforming into poliethnic communities, as well as changes in methodology of ethnic research.</p></div><br></div><div><h4>Keywords</h4><br><div>ethnicity; ethnos; plurality of interpretations; primordialism; constructivism
