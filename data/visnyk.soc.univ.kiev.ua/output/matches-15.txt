Definitions of ‘Socialization' Concept in Socio-Humanitarian Sciences
L. Kojekina
The peculiarities of interpretation of the notion "socialization" in terms of sociology, philosophy, and social psychology are analyzed. The interdisciplinarity of the notion is emphasized. It is concluded that the essence of socialization could be understood only when a person is regarded both as the object and the subject of social influence.</div><br></div><div><h4>Keywords</h4><br><div>socialization; personality; object and subject of social influence.
