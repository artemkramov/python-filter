Recent development strategies of social and religious organizations: sociological analysis
O. Ivankova-Stetsyuk
<p>The article attempts to outline specific features of the development&nbsp;strategy of&nbsp;modern&nbsp;social and religious organizations&nbsp;on&nbsp;the example of Caritas Ukraine&nbsp;as a&nbsp;«complex organization».&nbsp;&nbsp;By applying organizational&nbsp;(A. Etziony)&nbsp;and network (M. Newman) analysis,&nbsp;using data leitmotiv expert interviews&nbsp;the article describes practices of&nbsp;establishment of partnership networks of social and religious organizations.&nbsp;Benefits and risks of such kind organizations are evaluated. In this context, the network should be viewed as a structure that is formed between individuals or organizations, and displays various connections between them.Three types of partnership networks are detected, namely,&nbsp;networks of target interaction, networks of&nbsp;horizontal internal interaction and&nbsp;networks of&nbsp;horizontal external interaction.&nbsp;It is emphasized that the development of partnership networks of social and religious organizations is&nbsp;an actual strategy and a prerequisite for their development.</p><p>&nbsp;</p></div><br></div><div><h4>Keywords</h4><br><div>Development strategy; social and religious organizations; organizational analysis; network analysis; partnership network; expert interviews.
