Sociocultural milieu in the class structure of Ukrainian society
K. Taranova
<p>The article analyzes the concept of socio-cultural environments (milieu). Consider the basic theoretical principles of the concepts presented in&nbsp;the works of Michael Fester and Stefan Hradil. In the course of this review the conceptual construct of "sociocultural environment" (" milieu") was&nbsp;clarified. The article also presents the results of a research which attempts to apply the concept to the analysis of socio-cultural environments of&nbsp;Ukrainian society. The goal of the research was to determine the relationship between class (vertical ) and value (horizontal) structure of Ukrainian&nbsp;society, whereby detect signs of social and cultural environments in the community with the following interpretation of the formed milieu.</p></div><br></div><div><h4>Keywords</h4><br><div>socio-cultural environments; milieu; social structure; class structure; value grups; European Social Survey
