Social capital and rent-oriented social network under condition of state monopolism
O. Demkiv
<p>Author analyses the difficulties and possible ways of the of social capital theory adaptation to the conditions of the contemporary Ukrainian&nbsp;society. The peculiar features of Ukrainian society are analyzed using the concepts of "state monopolism", "rent", "rent-oriented social networks".<br><br></p></div><br></div><div><h4>Keywords</h4><br><div>social capital; state monopolism; rent; rent-oriented social networks
