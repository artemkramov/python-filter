Theoretical Approaches to the Study of Social Movements
L. Kaminska
The article is devoted to theoretiсal interpretation of social movements. The paradigms of social movements: collective behavior, mobilization of the resources, new social movements – are defined on the basis of which the mechanisms of their forming as well as their organizational features are described. The ways of structure and function changes of social movements in a modern society are singled out.</div><br></div><div><h4>Keywords</h4><br><div>collective behavior; social movements; recourses mobilization; collective identity; new social movements.
