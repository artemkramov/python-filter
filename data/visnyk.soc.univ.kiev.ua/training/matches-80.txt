Rationality in the late modern:development of methodology for understanding of onthology of the plurality
V. Shelukhin
<p>The problem of heuristic reconsideration of rationality based on theoretical approaches of N. Luhmann and A. Giddens is considered. The&nbsp;challenges for contemporary understanding of rationality deal with the problem of plurality. The author argues that concepts "communication" and&nbsp;"orientation" form the basic conceptual framework in analyses the problem of plural rationality and push the development of a new methodological&nbsp;approach to conceptualizing of the phenomenon of rationality.</p></div><br></div><div><h4>Keywords</h4><br><div>rationality; systems theory; structuration; communication; orientation
