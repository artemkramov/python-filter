An Approach to Personnel Selection-Related Decisions
Kadenko S.V., Tsyganok V.V.
An ordinal estimation experience-based method for selecting candidates, applying for vacancies, is suggested. A linear programming-based algorithm for defining personnel estimation criterion weights is described. A numerical example of the algorithm’s work is given. Tabl.: 1. Fig.: 2. Refs: 10 titles.
