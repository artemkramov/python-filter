Near-Field Probes for High-Density Optical Data Storage Systems Analysis
Denisyuk M.S.
State-of-the-art constructions of optical near-field probes are considered. Characteristics, technical features of using and ways of production of optical near-field probes for optical data storage are analyzed. The problems, solving of which will allow to increase optical data storage density up to 150 Gbit?cm–2 are determined. Fig.: 10. Refs: 43 titles.
