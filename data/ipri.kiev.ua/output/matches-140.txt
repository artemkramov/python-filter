Features of Solving Problems of Parameter Identification of Dynamic Systems in Conditions of Interval Uncertainty
Martsenyuk Ye.O.
The problem of parameter identification of linear dynamic systems by methods of analysis of interval data is considered. It is shown that in the case of taking into account the initial interval approximations of discrete values of the predicted characteristics, this problem is the problem of solving interval system of nonlinear algebraic equations. The features of formation and properties of the solution of such systems are investigated. Tabl.: 1. Fig.: 2. Refs: 7 titles.
