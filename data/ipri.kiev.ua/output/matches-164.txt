Conceptual Solutions of Creating an Automated System of Emergency Aid to the Population by a Single Phone Number «112»
Dodonov O.G., Koval O.V., Dzjubanenko R.I., Cepkov P.A., Zhydovlenko Ju.O., Mayurov M.O.
Conceptual solutions of creating an automated system for emergency aid to the population by a single phone number «112» in Ukraine is proposed. These solutions were implemented and tested at creating a territorial subsystem of the Government information-analytical system for emergency situations. Fig.: 1. Refs: 25 titles.
