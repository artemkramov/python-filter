Discrete Information Transfer by Stochastic Modulation of Random Carrier
Rozorynov G.N., Yegorov A.K., Bryagin O.V.
The original system of discrete information transfer, which provides the transmissions and optimum reception of stochastic modulated signals, is offered. The results of experimental research of the system, which confirm its realization and efficiency, are given. Fig.: 6. Refs.: 3 titles.
