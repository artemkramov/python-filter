Joint Processing of Trajectory Measuring Data at Testing the Complex Information-Control Systems
Dodonov А.G., Putyatin V.G., Valetchik V.А.
A method of trajectory measurements, which uses a joint processing of the measuring data, ob-tained from the proving ground means of external trajectory measurements and special onboard measuring equipment with the full-scale tests of the complex information-control systems at their constant disposition is considered.
