«Max-Min» Model for a Defense of Communication Networks Objects Problems
Dodonov О.G., Kuzmychov A.I.
The defense of strategic objects problem as a maximal flow/minimal cut in a network problem which is solved in the MS Excel by built-in module of mathematical optimization is interpreted. This approach is an illustration of modern method of forming and making responsible decisions with the use of mathematical and spreadsheet modeling. Fig.: 5. Refs: 10 titles.
communication network, maximal flow, minimal cut, spreadsheet modeling, mathematical optimization in MS Excel.
