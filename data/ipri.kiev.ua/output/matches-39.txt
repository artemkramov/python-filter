Retroreflection: Problems and Achievements
Petrov V.V., Shanoylo S.M., Antonov E.E., Granat O.I., Zenin V.N., Kravets V.G.
An analysis of retroreflective structures that are used in different branches of the national economy is conducted. The optimization method of characteristics of asymmetric retroreflective elements and the method for calculation of parameters of such elements are proposed. Optimum values of geometrical sizes of elements with maximal retroreflection for the given entrance angle are determined. Fig.: 4. Refs.: 10 titles.
