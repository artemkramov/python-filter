Multi-Criteria Decision-Making Support System with Expanded Capabilities
Totsenko V.G., Sigal T.G.
Construction of a multi-criteria decision-making support system with the expanded capabilities has been considered. The analysis of the existing decision-making support systems based on multi-criteria estimation has been carried out and the ways of their improvement has been found. The concept and structure of the system under discussion have been given. The system developed differs from existing ones in its capability of sharing quantitative and qualitative criteria for group determination of the im-portance of criteria and alternatives, and a capability of distribution of resources between alternatives.
