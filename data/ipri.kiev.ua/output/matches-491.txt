Survivability Mechanisms Application for Information Resource Security Providing in Dis-tributed Systems
Kuznetsova M.G.
Features of information resources security support in distributed information systems (DIS) are con-sidered. DIS’s survivability mechanisms are analyzed and the possibility of their application for security systems construction is demonstrated. Refs: 15 titles.
