Forming Technical Configuration of Computer-Aided Systems
Samokhvalov Yu.Ya., Naumenko E.M., Burba O.I.
One of the feasible approaches to formalization of the process for forming technical computer-aided systems configuration is considered. It allows to structure this process by sequence of intercommunication technological procedures and to obtain necessary and sufficient source data for the substantiation of requirements to the system.
