Face Image Verification in the Space of Invariant Features Based on Trace Transforms
Gorohovatsky A.V.
The problem of human face image verification is studied. A method of processing invariant features for recognition on the basis of analysing the image projection functions systems is proposed. The conducted experiments confirm high speed and normal operation of an approach. Tabl.: 1. Fig.: 2. Refs: 6 titles.
computer vision, recognition, face verification, invariant features, projection transform, Trace/Radon.
