The Realization of Convolution with Recursive Procedures for 3D-Reconstruction Algorithms
Zakidalsky A.I., Tsibulskaya E.A.
A method of calculation of convolution, based on the application of fast Fourier transform (FFT) is considered. Variants of program realization of convolution are resulted. It is shown that the use of FFT allows to increase the calculation efficiency of convolution and to reduce the time of its performance. Tabl.: 1. Refs.: 5 titles.
3D-reconstruction, convolution, fast Fourier transform.
