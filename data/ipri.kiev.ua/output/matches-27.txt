Methods of the Multilayer Photoluminescent Information Recording
Belyak Ye.V., Kravets V.G., Kryuchyn A.A.
The possibility of composite organic and inorganic luminescent materials using for media synthesis is analyzed. Experimental results got a theoretical explanation based on the energy level structure of the luminescent dye. Complex modifications of the optical disc information layer and data reader for multi-layer photoluminescent media creating are proposed. Tabl.: 1. Fig.: 5. Refs.: 10 titles.
