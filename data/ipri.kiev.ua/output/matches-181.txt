Feature Hierarchies in Structural Methods of Image Analysis
Gorohovatsky V.A.
Questions of information processing in computer vision intellectual systems are discussed. With the purpose of increasing the efficiency of image analysis a hierarchical approach to construction of features and measures for comparison of the structural descriptions is proposed. Results of computer modelling are given. Fig.: 1. Refs: 7 titles.
computer vision, structural image recognition methods, characteristic features, hierarchy, relations, noise immunity.
