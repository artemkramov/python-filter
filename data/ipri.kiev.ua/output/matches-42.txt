The Use of Simulation Modeling for Finding the Internal Maximum Stream in a Transport Network of a Region
Maksimey I.V., Sukach E.I., Giruts P.V.
The use of a generalized simulation model of a regional transport network is offered. It takes into consideration the influence of casual internal streams and a probable ageing of the roads for finding a maximum stream in a network and determining «bottle-necks» in a network. Refs.: 4 titles.
