Program Complex for Modeling Complex Systems on the Basis of Process Algebra
Nesterenko B.B., Novotarskiy M.A
A necessity of application of process algebra as a formal means for the description of complex sys-tems is proved. Basic concepts of construction of a program complex which is based on the expanded process algebra are considered. Principles of the preprocessor language organization for process algebra and a subsystem for simulations are described. Comparison of the suggested program complex with known similar environments for simulations is carried out. Tabl.: 1. Fig.: 3. Refs.: 10 titles.
