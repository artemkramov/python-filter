Comparative Analysis of Information Exchange Procedures in the Telecommunication Systems
Маtоv О.Ya., Vasylenkо V.S.
The comparative analysis of information exchange procedures in the telecommunication systems by the indexes of relative and effective speed, and by the validity of information transfer for information exchange protocols in the telecommunication systems is offered; expressions for the calcu-lation of these indexes are given.
