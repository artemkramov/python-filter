Probabilistic Model to Identify Latent Connections in Networks of Concepts
Dodonov O.G., Lande D.V.
A model to identify implicit (latent) connections in the networks of concepts within the concept of complex networks is offered. As an example, a network of concepts (companies) which communicate with each other through joint references in the Web publications is considered. Today the considered di rection of analysis of complex networks is relevant when making-decisions in such areas as marketing, social research, competitive intelligence. Fig.: 6. Refs: 17 titles.
