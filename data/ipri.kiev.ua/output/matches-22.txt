Estimation of reliability of highly reliable systems with the account spare parts.
Kovalenko I.N., Butochnov A.N.
The approached top and bottom estimations of factor of readiness of highly reliable restored system with structural redundancy are offered. The received formulas can be used for an estimation of reliability of highly reliable systems in view of various strategy of updating spare parts.
