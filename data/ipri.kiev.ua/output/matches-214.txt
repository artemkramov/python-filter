Generalized Architecture of Аnalytical Сomponent of Сorporate Information-Analytical Systems
Koval О.V.
Generalized architecture of analytical component of corporate information-analytical systems (CIAS) is proposed. It represents its main components for solving Business Analytics tasks depending on the functional purpose. The main directions of development of software tools of analytical component of the CIAS on the basis of Business Analytics tasks currently are defined. Fig.: 5. Refs: 24 titles.
