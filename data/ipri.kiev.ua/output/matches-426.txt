Actual problems of long-term preservation of documentation in insurance fund of documentation of Ukraine
Podorozhnyi V.I.
The directions in which the research is conducted to ensure long-term preservation of copies of documents in the insurance fund of documentation of Ukraine are presented. It is shown that in the state system of insurance fund documentation, based on the SRI micrography, has technical and technological capabilities to create electronic copies of documentation that are designed for electronic storage and to create the insurance fund on microfilms.
