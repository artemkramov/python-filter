Mathematical Models of the Productivity Losses Caused by Conflicts at the System of Mediators of the Shared Ontology in a GRID Environment
Matov O.Ya.
The types of possible conflicts are defined and analytical models for research of productivity losses coefficient values in the system of mediators of the shared ontology in a GRID environment for structures as follow: homogeneous and heterogeneous libraries, in cases of absence and presence of high-productivity caches, for single- and multi-port libraries are developed.
