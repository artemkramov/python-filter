Optimization of Using Network Scientific Information Resources of Foreign Publishing Houses
Simonenko T.V.
Possibilities for expansion of access to full texts of scientific electronic magazines of leading pub-lishing houses of the world are considered. The attention to necessities of association of telecommunica-tion access to external information resources with formation of collections of the scientific periodical press on compact discs in Ukraine is stressed. The approach to definition of an optimum ratio between these ways of access to electronic publications is proved. The approach is based on law of concentration and dispersion of the information.
