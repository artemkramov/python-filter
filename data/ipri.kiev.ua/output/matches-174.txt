The Analysis of the Reliability of Mechanical Systems by Using a Method of Probability-Algebraic Simulation
Sukach E.I.
An approach to research of characteristics of the reliability of mechanical systems, considering the probability nature of accumulation of damages by components of a system and statistical character of interference of damages in the process of system service is described. A method of probability-algebraic simulation of reliability characteristics of functionally complex systems by reliability characteristics of components making them up is offered. Fig.: 4.
