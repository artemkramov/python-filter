Strategy of Innovative Activity on the Basis of Technology Foresight Methodology
Zgurovsky M.Z., Pankratova N.D.
Methodology of scenario analysis, realization of which is based on successive implementation of the basic stages of technological foresight process, is given. It allows to form the integral process of foresight and develop a group of scenarios of the future behaviour of a research object. On the basis of the offered methodology the exposure of critical technologies priorities by Power Engineering and Power Efficiency direction in Ukraine is conducted. Tabl.: 2. Fig.: 3.
