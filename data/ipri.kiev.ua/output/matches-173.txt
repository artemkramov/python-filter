Problem of Resource Allocation as an Extension of Decision Support Systems Capabilities
Tsyganok V.V.
A method of solving the problem of resource allocation using decision support systems is proposed. The problem after discretization is solved using a modified genetic algorithm. The method is implemented for practical use in the «Solon-3» decision support system. Fig.: 4. Refs: 4 titles.
