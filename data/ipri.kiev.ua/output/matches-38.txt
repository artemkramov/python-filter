Methods of Stability Study for Decisions, Obtained by Using Goal Method
Tsyganok V.V.
Methods of decisions’ stability study (expert’s errors independence) in the Goal-type Decision Sup-port Systems are described. Ranking Decision Stability — order preservation in alternative ranking under natural expert’s errors during expert estimation, and Estimating Decision Stability — holding estimations within the bounds of specified maximal relative inaccuracy, are considered. The task of choosing expert estimation method for Decision Stability Support while using specific Goal-type Knowledge Base is solved. Tabl.: 2. Fig.: 3. Refs.: 6 titles.
