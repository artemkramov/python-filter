The Normalized Form of Representation of the Exponential Function in Commutative Hypercomplex Number Systems
Kalinovsky Ja.A., Bojarinova Ju.E.
The normalized form of representation of the exponential function in the commutative hypercomplex number systems (HNSs), its structure, methods of construction and capabilities of use for solving the problem of determining the isomorphism of various HNSs is considered. Tabl.: 1. Refs: 5 titles.
hypercomplex number system, exponential, characteristic equation, isomorphism, multiple roots.
