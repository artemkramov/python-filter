Macroeconomic Factors Analysis Tools
Boychenko A.V., Senchenko V.R.
An approach to creating the macroeconomic factors monitoring system is considered. A complex of technological solutions to provide the analysis and visualization of data for this subject domain is offered. Features of facilities for visual analysis of statistical data and data logical model developed within the scope of the monitoring system is presented. Fig.: 8. Refs.: 10 titles.
