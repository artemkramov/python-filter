Methodology of the Situation Control in the Automated Systems for Providing a Necessary Level of Information Security of State Power
Nesterenko O.V.
The question of the formalized statement of a problem of the situation control in the conditions of functioning in the public authorities of the proper automated information-analytical systems for providing run-time firmness of fulfillment of the entrusted functions by the public authority, contribution to fight against information entropy, expansion of access to information in the process of decision-making by experts and governing body as a substantial factor of providing information security of public authority is considered.
