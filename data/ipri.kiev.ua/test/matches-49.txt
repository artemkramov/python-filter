Decision Support on Information Security Incidents Management in Organizational-Technical Systems
Gladysh S.V.
The automation and intellectualization problem of information security incidents management in organizational-technical systems is considered. The research goal, factors, criteria and parameters of au-tomated decision support procedures are defined. The functional scheme of the corresponding intelli-gence system is proposed. Fig.: 1.
