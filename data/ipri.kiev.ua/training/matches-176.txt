The Determination of the Earth's Magnetic Field Horizontal Components Using Magnetometers of an Electronic Compass
Raievskyi N.V., Morozov Yu.V.
The procedure of determining horizontal components of the earth's magnetic field using magnetometers is implemented. It is necessary to compensate the destabilizing factors effect on sensitive elements to determine the true values of the quantities. The compensation of destabilizing factors effect is performed by calibration. To calculate an azimuth and to determine the position of an object in real time the real components of the magnetic field are used. Fig.: 4. Refs.: 11 titles.
