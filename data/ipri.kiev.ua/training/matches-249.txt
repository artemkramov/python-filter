Formation and Prospects of Development of CD Manufacturing in Ukraine
Masol I.V.
The analysis of technologies for manufacturing CD substrates is given. The results of analyzing the influence of polycarbonate injection molding parameters on the quality of CD substrates are present-ed. The main parameters of the injection molding process are determined. They allow to create high-quality CDs. Tabl.: 1. Fig.: 5. Refs: 11 titles.
