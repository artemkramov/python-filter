The Automation of Data Processing and Analyzing for the Simulation of the Railway System
Sukach E.I.
The facilities for automation of simulation of the railway system functioning in the conditions of random actions are described. A technique of definition of the most effective variant of the network organization allowing to coordinate in time and in space to moving of cargo transport streams is offered. Fig.: 1.
