Optoelectronic Data Stream Processing Devices
Danilov V.V., Lipinsky A.Y., Rudiakova A.N.
The analogous and discrete optoelectronic acousto-optic stream data processing devices such as the acousto-optic correlators/convolvers with the eight bit equivalent precision and optoelectronic acousto-optic processors with much higher precision are considered. The mathematical description of the signal transformation processes by the discrete optoelectronic acousto-optic devices is presented. Fig.: 2. Refs: 5 titles.
data stream processing, acousto-optic correlator/convolver, discrete acousto-optic processor, optoelectronic devices for discrete data processing.
