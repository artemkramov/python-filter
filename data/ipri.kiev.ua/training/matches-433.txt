Construction of Some Functions in the 4th Order Hypercomplex Numerical System
Sinkov M.V., Boyarinova Y.E., Kalinovsky J.A., Fedorenko A.V.
Questions of construction of such inverse functions as the logarithm, hyperbolic and trigonometri-cal arc sine and arc cosine are considered. Examples of construction of inverse functions for the 4th order hypercomplex numerical system are given. Refs: 9 titles.
