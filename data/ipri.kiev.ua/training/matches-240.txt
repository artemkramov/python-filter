Aggregating Group Expert Estimates Obtained in Different Scales
Tsyganok V.V.
To facilitate obtaining data from an expert in decision-making support systems, it is suggested to use pair comparison method, providing the expert with an opportunity to conduct estimation in a scale, which is just detailed enough to reflect his(her) competency in the problem under consideration. In order to aggregate expert estimates obtained in this way a method, taking into account different importance of estimates obtained in different scales, is suggested. It is assumed that the importance of each estimate is proportional to expert’s competence in a specific issue and to his confidence in the judgment expressed. It is suggested to use feedback with experts in order to increase the consistency level of the estimates they provided. Fig.: 2. Refs: 16 titles.
