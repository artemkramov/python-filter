Test Plan of Hardware Write Blockers for Computer Forensics Applications
Kozhenevsky S.R., Prokopenko S.D.
The principles of design of modern write blockers for computer forensics applications are considered. The requirements for such devices are elaborated. The common principles of carrying-out the tests are developed. The test plan of hardware write blockers with SATA and PATA interfaces ia offered. It can be applied when carrying-out certification testing. Tab.: 1. Fig.: 2. Refs: 6 titles.
