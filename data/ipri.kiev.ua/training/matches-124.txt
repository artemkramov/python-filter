Mathematical Simulation of the Fluorescent Multilayer Media Readout Process
Belak E.V.
Task of volumetric media effective configuration has been analyzed. Mathematical model which allows to bring into correlation by means of software tools fluorescent multilayer disc parameters with readout signal and signal-to-noise ratio is proposed. Optimal geometry of recording layer elements of the media is estimated. Fig.: 4. Refs: 8 titles.
