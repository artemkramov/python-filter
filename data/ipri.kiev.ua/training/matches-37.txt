An Approach to an Estimation of Efficiency of the Automated Aystems on Early Design Stages
Naumenko E.M., Kozlov N.S.
An approach to an estimation of efficiency of the automated system on early design stages in conditions of significant uncertainty of the information environment is offered. Practical use of the suggested approach allows to make the proved decisions on expediency of creation of a system and its expected efficiency. Tabl.: 1. Fig.: 2. Refs.: 5 titles.
