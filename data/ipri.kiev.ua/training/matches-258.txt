Adaptation of Distributed Information Infrastructures for Government Bodies Interaction
Khramova I.О.
An approach to improve the adaptive properties of the distributed information infrastructure components to evolutionary changes in the operating environment, using modern information techno-logies is under consideration. The approach can be used to create a joint information space from existing stand-alone information resources for government bodies to solve complex multidisciplinary problems. Tabl.: 1.
