Сonstruct of Gigh-Dimension Hypercomplex Number Systems Using the Dimension Multiplication Procedure
Boyarinova Yu.E.
The construction of new high-dimension hypercomplex number systems using the dimension multiplication procedure is studied. This allows to construct low- and high-filled multiplication tables of isomorphic hypercomplex number systems, which can be used in the synthesis of digital filters. The properties of the dimension multiplication procedure are considered. Refs.: 8 titles
hypercomplex number systems, table of multiplication, dimension multiplication procedure.
