The Organization of Information Proxy Servers Network
Dodonov A.G., Lande D.V.
Problems of the modern web-space which prevent to consider it as a reliable and viable information resource are given. The approach to the organization of the network infrastructure allowing to solve a set of problems based on use of content-monitoring systems and information proxies network is described. Fig.: 2. Internet resources, information proxy server, reliability, data availability, information network in-frastructure, web content.
