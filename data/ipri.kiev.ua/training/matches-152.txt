Direct Method of Forming Surface Relief Gratings in Chalcogenide Glass Film
Rubish V.M., Trunov M.L., Lytvyn P.M., Gera E.V., Tarnay A.A., Rigan M.Yu., Petrachenko M.Ye.
Kinetics of the growth of surface relief in binary chalcogenide systems films by atomic-force microscopy technique is investigated. The optimal film compositions and the recording modes of surface relief gratings are determined. The mechanism of relief forming is discussed. Fig.: 5. Refs: 18 titles.
