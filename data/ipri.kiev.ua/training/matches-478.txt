Adaptive Search in Scientific Libraries Information-Search Systems
Hadzhinov V.V., Yakovleva Yu.V.
Traditional information search adaptation methods are analyzed. Methods which are expedient for using in information retrieval systems of scientific libraries are selected. Directions on using search adap-tation method on the basis of user request modification are offered. Fig.: 1. Refs: 14 titles.
