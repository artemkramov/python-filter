Methodical Aspects of the Conceptual Design of Automated Systems
Samokhvalov Yu.Ya., Naumenko E.M., Burba O.I.
An approach to structuring the process of conceptual design of automated systems has been considered. There are offered the methods of formalization of the stage of system conception variants, choice a variant that best takes into account user requirements, and the expected results of the selected variant.
