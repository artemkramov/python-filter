Restoration of Information in a Secret Sharing Problem for the Second Order Hypercomplex Numerical Systems with the Help of Euclidian Algorithm
Boyarinova Yu.E., Odarich Ya.V.
Examples of restoration of a secret by using Euclidian algorithm at representing information in the second order hypercomplex numerical systems are considered. Refs.: 8 titles.
Euclidian algorithm, secret sharing problem, complex numbers, double numbers, dual numbers.
