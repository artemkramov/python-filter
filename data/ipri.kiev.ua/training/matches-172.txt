Computer Information Mass Distribution Technologies
Kozheshkurt V.I., Petrov I.V., Berezin B.O., Stetsenko A.M., Solonina N.V., Lesnov V.O.
The features of computer information mass distribution technologies are analyzed. The principles of construction, examples of introduction and characteristics of an automated computer information mass distribution system developed and introduced at the Institute for Information Recording of NAS of Ukraine are considered. Refs: 9 titles.
computer information mass distribution, electronic computer newspaper, information services, push-technology, corporate network.
