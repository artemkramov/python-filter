«Problems of horizontal informative resources integration for multilevel organizational structures with dynamic configuration».
A.Matov, I.Khramova
Specific features and possibilities of integration technologies application for dynamic combination of numerous information resources with different organizational subordination in the context of information infrastructure of decision-making support are considered. Solutions which extend ability of information infrastructure to answer expectation of users in part of granting information from the aggregate of sources relevant to the solvable problem are offered.
information infrastructure, decision-making support, information resources integration, semantic metadata.
