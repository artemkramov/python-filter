Synthesis Methodology of Hardware-Software Complex Architecture of the Automated Con-ditions Monitoring System
Kozheshkurt V.I., Lutsik S.L., Smertenko E.V.
The approach to designing architecture of the information processing complex of the automated real time conditions monitoring system based on classification of functional tasks on the basis of methods of cluster analysis and the chosen set of similarity attributes is offered. The developed approach allows to allocate from a set of functions the systems similar (on certain attributes) and to unite them in architectural components (unified functional modules). Tabl.: 1.
