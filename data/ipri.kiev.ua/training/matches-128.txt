Tomographic Reconstruction of «Large» Objects Using Partial Scans
Zakydalskiy А.I., Tsibulska Е.O.
The tomographic reconstruction algorithms for «large» objects are presented. The cases of using partial scans of horizontal and vertical sections if the objects sizes are greater by several fold than the angle of cone-beam and the matrix detector sizes are considered. The results of the tomographic reconstruction modeling using the mathematical phantoms are given. Fig.: 4. Refs: 19 titles.
