Analysis of Methods for Fabrication of Near-Field Optical Fiber Probes for Superdense In-formation Recording Systems
Goldayevich Е E.
The following methods for near-field optical fiber probes fabrication as a mechanical heating-pulling method, chemical etching methods, photoplastic near-field optical probe replication from nano-mould fabrication method, and combined methods are considered. The focused ion-beam milling method for optical fiber probe tapered tip aperture forming is considered. Fig.: 6. Refs: 6 titles.
