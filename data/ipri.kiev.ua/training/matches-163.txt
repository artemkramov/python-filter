The Main Directions of Creating Automated Systems for Real-Time Monitoring Air, Ground and Above-Water Space
Petrov V.V., Kozheshkurt V.I., Butochnov A.N., Naumenko E.M., Ostashevsky V.B.
The basic directions of creating automated systems for monitoring a set of dynamic objects are considered. In some detail techniques of structural and functional design of monitoring systems, the definition of software architecture of information processing centres and evaluation of the expected efficiency on the automation of functions for collection and processing monitoring information are presented.
