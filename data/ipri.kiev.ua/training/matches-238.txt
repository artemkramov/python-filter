Features of Data Acquisition and Recovery from Modern Digital Video Recorders
Kozhenevsky S.R., Prokopenko S.D.
The article is dedicated to the problem of data acquisition and recovery from modern digital video recorders (DVR). Information about DVR design and principles of operation is given. The solution to the problem of gaining access to video records is presented. Methods of data acquisition and recovery from DVRs and their features are analyzed. Practical advices about the organization of data acquisition from DVRs are given.
