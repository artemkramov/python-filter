Three-Level Project Management Corporate System
Kuzminykh V.A., Khaustov D.V., Korostelova Ye.Yu.
The questions of risk management in a project management corporate system are considered. An approach to creation of quantitative estimation of risks of implementation of projects and on its basis of determination of quality estimation of the state of project and possible operations for the removal of negative consequences from the influence of risks is proposed. Tabl.: 3. Fig.: 2. Refs: 5 titles.
project management corporate system, risk management, analysis of influence of risks.
