Modern Retrieval Technologies — Problems and Some Ways of their Solution
Dodonov A.G., Lande D.V., Putyatin V.G.
Main problems, arising at development of modern retrieval technologies, such as: necessity of covering large volumes of information, large and complex dynamics of information streams, frequent duplication of information, excess of noise information, presence of the hidden web-space, absence of the real model of web-space etc are described. Approaches to the solution of these problems and directions of development of retrieval technologies are offered. Fig.: 2. Refs: 25 titles.
Internet, information retrieval system, information stream, WWW, model of webspace.
