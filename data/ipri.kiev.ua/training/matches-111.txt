A method for the optimisation of algorithms for deciding of linear equation systems with a corrupted right part over the residue ring modulo 2N
Aleksejchuk А.N., Ignatenko S.M.
A method of constructing new algorithms for deciding of linear equation systems with a corrupted right part over the residue ring modulo 2N, from any finite aggregate of old such algorithms, is proposed. It is shown that, in certain accidents, the proposed method can the efficiency of well knowning methods for deciding of such linear equation systems essential elevate. Refs: 16 titles.
correlation cryptanalysis, system of linear equations with a corrupted right part, optimisation of computation algorithms
