Surface Plasmon Wave Modeling Method for Tree-Dimensional Curved Surfaces
Denisyuk M.S.
FDTD-method modification for 3D-domain is proposed. This modification increases accuracy of electric and magnetic fields vectors calculation on dielectric-dielectric and metal-dielectric interfaces. It allows modeling processes of excitation and propagation of surface plasmon waves for curved metaldielectric interfaces. Fig.: 7. Refs: 6 titles.
