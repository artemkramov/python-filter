The Ways of Improving Optical Characteristics of Microprism Structures
Petrov V.V., Shanoylo S.M., Antonov E.E., Lapchuk А.S.
An analysis of the ways of improving optical characteristics for microprism retroreflective structures that are used in ophthalmology and road industry is conducted. The technologies for forming microprism relief on transparent optical materials are considered. The methods of spectral characteristics optimization and chromatic phenomenon compensation for retroreflective structures of sight correction are investigated. Fig.: 7. Refs: 6 titles.
