Minimization of Calculations in the Algorithm of Volume Reconstruction
Zakidalsky A.I.
It is suggested to use preliminary interpolation of convolute projection data during realization of in-verted projection in the algorithm of volume reconstruction. Analysis results of difficulty of different ways for interpolation realization are prеsented, the estimation of error because of presence in the spec-trum of a signal of constituents higher than Nyquist frequency is given. It is suggested to use the truncated sinc-interpolation with Lanczos window, to provide exactness comparable with interpolation on the basis of the fast Fourier transform, but with computational complexity by an order lesser.
