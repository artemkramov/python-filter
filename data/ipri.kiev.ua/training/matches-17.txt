SURVIVABILITY OF INFORMATIONAL-ANALYTICAL SYSTEMS:CONSEPTUAL BASIS, MODELS FOR ANALYSIS AND EVALUATION
Dodonov A.G., Gorbachyk O.S., Kuznyetsova M.G.
Information-analytical system’s features as the components of up-to-date information infrastructure are observed. Concepts of functional, structural, information survivability are introduced. Models for analysis and evaluation are given.
