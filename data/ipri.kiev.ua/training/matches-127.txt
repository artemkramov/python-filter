An Algebraic Characteristic of the Partially Recursive Graph Functions Class
Snigur N.N.
The class of all computable functions depending on finite graphs is considered. A generating set for the algebra of partially recursive graph functions is determined, and it is also proved that this set is complete. Refs: 15 titles.
