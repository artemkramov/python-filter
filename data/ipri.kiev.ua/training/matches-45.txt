Restoration and Reconstruction of a Sound Signal in Soundtracks of Rarity Record Carriers
Kosyak I.V.
An algorithm for digital processing of sound signals read from phonocylinders is proposed and an estimation of influence of performing restoration procedures is carried out. The presence in soundtracks of sound distortion is investigated and the types of noise existing in them are classified. Fig.: 2. Refs.: 8 titles.
