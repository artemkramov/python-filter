Adaptive Filtration of Parameters of Maneuvering Object Movement in Rectangular Coordinate System
Zhuk S.Y., Kozheshkurt V.I., Yuzefovych V.V.
The optimal and quasi-optimal algorithms of adaptive filtration of parameters of maneuvering object movement in rectangular coordinate system on the basis of the mixed Markov processes theory in discrete time are synthesized. The analysis of quasi-optimal algorithm is executed by a method of statistical modeling on the computer on a test example. Fig.: 6. Refs: 5 titles.
