Optoelectronic Acousto-Optical Digital-to-Analog Converter
Rudiakova A.N., A.Y. Lipinskу, Danilov V.V.
While solving the problems of effective data processing by the optoelectronic systems, the great at-tention is paid to the realization of digital and analog devices, characteristics of which can substantial by influence on the speed of such systems. A functional model of optoelectronic acousto-optical digital-to-analog converter that is based on the simultaneous space-and-time integration principle is presented. The modeling of such device operation is performed using the Simulink package. Fig.: 14. Refs.: 14 titles.
