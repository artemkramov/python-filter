Estimation of Reliability of Multipurpose Software-Hardware Complexes
Butochnov A.N, Avgustovsky V.V., Tsitsarev V.N.
A technique and algorithms for calculation of approximate values of parameters of reliability (average time between failures and average time of restoration) for multipurpose software-hardware complexes are offered. They allow to take into account both failures of hardware, and the failures generated by errors in programs. Fig.: 4. Refs.: 5 titles.
