Retroreflector Elements Characteristics Improvement Using Measurement and Correction of a Cutter Angle
Borodin Y.A., Lapchuk A.S., Shyhovets A.V.
Angle characteristics of microprism retroreflectors are analyzed. The original method of measurement and correction of a cutter angle to maximize optical efficiency of reflected back light is proposed. The different original schemes and examples of cutter angle measurement are presented. Tabl.: 2. Fig.: 10. Refs: 8 titles.
