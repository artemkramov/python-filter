Investigation of Data Interchange Protocols of Modern PCs and Hard Disk Drives
Kozhenevsky S.R., Prokopenko S.D.
Actual commands that PC exchanges with HDD are investigated. Experiments on various software and hardware platforms are conducted. Here particular attention was given to commands modifying data on hard disks. Theoretical principles of interaction between PCs and HDDs are presented. Common description of ATA interface standard and command sets used in it is given. Tabl.: 4. Fig.: 2. Refs: 7 titles.
