Justification of the Long-Term Storage Electronic Documents Archives Firmware Choice
Petrov I.V., Stetsenko A.M., Solonina N.V.
The results of researches of formation processes of relief nanostructures by using methods of inorganic laser photolithography are presented. It is shown that the application of inorganic photoresists on the basis of chalcogenide vitreous semiconductors for forming relief nanostructures with the sizes of tens of nanometers is promising, experience of using of inorganic photoresists in the process of CD mastering is summarized.
