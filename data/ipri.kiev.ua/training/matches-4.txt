Algorithm of Testing the Hypothesis about Normality of a Random Vector
Mukan O.V., Vasilenko V.A., Yegorov O.A., Arteev T.M.
The algorithm of testing the hypothesis about Gaussian distribution of two-dimensional random vector is considered. The cumulative test as a measure of a divergence of selective and hypothetical dis-tribution in algorithm is used. Results of research of dependence of capacity of cumulative test from vol-ume of sample are given. The way of increasing efficiency of cumulative test is offered. Fig.: 5. Refs.: 5 titles.
