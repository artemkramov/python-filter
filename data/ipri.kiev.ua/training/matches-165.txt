Virtual Human Movements Modeling for Dimensional Sign Language Reproduction
Kryvonos Yu.G., Krak Yu.V., Barmak O.V.
A new approach of modeling movements of sign language for further dimensional reproduction using 3Dmodel is proposed. The ability of the proposed technology to reproduce quite realistically the motion obtained from the native sign language speaker recorded on video using a three-dimensional model is shown. Fig.: 3.
