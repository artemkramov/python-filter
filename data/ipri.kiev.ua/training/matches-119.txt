Information Resources Protection
Smirnova N.А., Gaidargi V.I.
The classification of existent security threats of web-portal is considered. An overview of existent control management systems (CMS) and their components — security systems is given. Main part is dedicated to designing the universal high-level security system, which is intended for providing resource protection from most types of attacks. Fig.: 1. Refs: 9 titles.
