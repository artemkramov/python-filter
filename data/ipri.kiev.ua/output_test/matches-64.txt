Method of Generalization of Thematic Cards by a Colour Analysis
Gaydarzhy V.I., Zinchuk A.Ya.
Main destination of thematic cards is providing of users with spatially-localized thematic infor-mation about a natural environment and socio-economic objects and phenomena for the solution of national-economic and scientific problems, and also for the general aims of cognition of the world around. Technology based on a visual analysis and generalization of graphic image of thematic cards is considered. The presence of a set of simple thematic cards on the basis of which one can obtain the generalized card by using one of considered methods of colour analysis is assumed. Fig.: 3. Refs.: 8 titles.
