/div><div>Research in the relationship between the architecture and function of proteins aims to understand the mechanism<br><br>of proteins folding and their activity at the atomic level. This knowledge leads to a progress in new technologies<br><br>that allow simultaneous production of numerous proteins on a large scale – high throughput protein production<br><br>(HTPP) methods. The HTPP methods allow parallel processing of multiple samples and are also important for<br><br>the determination of optimal protein production procedure, where usually combinations of different conditions<br><br>have to be tested. This article describes available cloning, expression, and purification approaches that may be<br><br>used in a high throughput and parallel manner. Additionally, the implementation of automation facilities is briefly<br><br>outlined.<
