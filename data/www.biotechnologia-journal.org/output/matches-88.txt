/div><div>In this report, we have presented the analysis of the enzymatic methods of protoplasts formation of Cunninghamella echinulata. Hydrolytic enzymes of different origin (i.e. prokaryotic and eukaryotic) such as chitinase (EC.3.2.1.14), lyticase (mix of lytic enzymes), and proteinase K (EC 3.4.21.64) were used for fungal cell wall disruption. An effective protoplast formation was achieved with the following enzyme solutions: chitinase (0.33 U A ml!1), proteinase K (1.8-3.6 U A ml!1), and lyticase (160 UA ml!1). Fluorescent microscopy was used to evaluate the ability of the tested enzymes to effectively produce protoplasts.<
