/div><div>The aim of the present study is to determine the influence of different concentrations of growth regulators on<br><br>the hypocotyl explants regeneration abilities of flax cultivars (<i>Linum usitatissimum</i>L.). Plant material used in<br><br>the study was obtained from two flax cultivars: Modran and Selena. Hypocotyl explants were obtained from 6-dayold<br><br>seedlings and were subsequently placed on basal MS (Murashige and Skoog 1962) medium and MS medium<br><br>supplemented with 6-benzylaminopurine (BAP) or naftalene acetic acid (NAA). Final evaluation of the callus,<br><br>the shoot and the root formation of the explants was performed 28 days after the experimental setup. The highest<br><br>regeneration effectiveness was observed on the media supplemented with 1 mg/l BAP.<
