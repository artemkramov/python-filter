/div><div>An overview of recent studies conducted with the use of various plant tissues, organ culture and plant regeneration<br><br>of<i>Artemisia</i>species is presented in this paper. Species that belong to this genus contain important secondary<br><br>metabolites of diverse structure and biological activity. Owing to their valuable pharmacological properties,<br><br><i>Artemisia</i>species have been the object of various biotechnological studies. Different<i>in vitro</i>methods of<i>Artemisia</i><br><br>sp. culture are key to the production of these valuable compounds – especially sesquiterpene lactones – artemisinin<br><br>and arglabin – used as natural drugs.<
